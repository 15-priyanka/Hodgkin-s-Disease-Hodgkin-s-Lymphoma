Hodgkin's Disease/Hodgkin's Lymphoma

" This is a type of cancer of the lymphatic system"

SYMPTOMS:

  • night sweats
  • itchy skin
  • fever
  • fatigue
  • unintended weight loss
  • persistent cough, trouble breathing, chest pain
  • pain in the lymph nodes after consuming alcohol
  • enlarged spleen

CAUSES:

  • second cancers
  • infertility
  • infections
  • thyroid problems
  • lung damage
